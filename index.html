About Me

I'm a junior level designer focused on creating gameplay spaces that combine combat encounters and player exploration. My work explores how level layout, visual cues, and environmental design guide player behavior and shape moment-to-moment experience. I'm particularly interested in how spaces communicate with players without explicit instruction. I have experience building levels and gameplay prototypes using Unreal Engine and Unity, and I enjoy analyzing existing games through level design and gameplay breakdowns.

I'm currently studying Computation Arts at Concordia University.
