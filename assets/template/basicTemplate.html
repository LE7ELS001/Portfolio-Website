Overview

This project explores how an industrial environment can support combat encounters and player exploration through readable space, clear progression, and controlled player routing.

Design Goal

The goal of this level is to create a space that encourages forward movement while maintaining tension through enemy placement, environmental framing, and route clarity.

Process

The level started from a simple blockout focused on testing room scale, traversal flow, and sightlines.

After establishing the rough layout, I iterated on encounter positions, cover placement, and route readability.

Current work is focused on refining pacing and improving environmental communication.
